More about Hans Zimmer

Hans Zimmer has composed music for the following movies:

  • Gladiator (2000)
  • Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl (2003)
  • The Dark Knight Trilogy
    • Batman Begins (2005)
    • The Dark Knight (2008)
    • The Dark Knight Rises (2012)
  • Inception (2010)
  • Interstellar (2014)
  • The Lion King (2019) - Live-action adaptation
  • Dunkirk (2017)
  • The Da Vinci Code (2006)
  • Sherlock Holmes (2009) and Sherlock Holmes: A Game of Shadows (2011)
  • Man of Steel (2013)
  • The Pirates of the Caribbean sequels
    • Dead Man's Chest (2006)
    • At World's End (2007)
    • On Stranger Tides (2011)
    • Dead Men Tell No Tales (2017)
  • Rain Man (1988)
  • Blade Runner 2049 (2017)
  • The Last Samurai (2003)
  • Pearl Harbor (2001)
  • The Thin Red Line (1998)
  • The Rock (1996)
  • The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014)
